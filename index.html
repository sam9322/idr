Who We Are

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to closing the global skills gap in digital, cyber, and technology risk.

Operating in regulated, high-consequence environments, we bridge the divide between theoretical insights and practical application through key strategic partnerships with leading UK universities. By uniting academic excellence with front-line industry experience, IDR delivers transformative real-world projects and robust talent pipelines.
